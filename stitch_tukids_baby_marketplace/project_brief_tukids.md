# Project Brief: TuKids - The Modern Baby Marketplace

## 1. Project Vision
TuKids is a modern, minimalist, and intuitive marketplace specializing in baby products (diapers, strollers, clothing, toys, cribs, etc.). It serves as a dual-sided platform where both official stores and individual parents can sell new and pre-loved items, fostering a trusted "family-to-family" community.

---

## 2. Target Audience
*   **New & Expecting Parents:** Looking for high-quality essentials and guidance.
*   **Eco-conscious Families:** Interested in sustainable, second-hand options.
*   **Official Baby Brands & Boutiques:** Seeking a dedicated, niche marketplace.

---

## 3. Visual Identity & Branding
*   **Mascot:** "Tuki," a stylized, friendly baby toucan with a soft, colorful beak.
*   **Palette (Tender Modernity):**
    *   **Primary:** Coral (#FF8C7A) - CTAs and branding.
    *   **Secondary:** Mint Pastel (#7DD8C7) - Success states and secondary actions.
    *   **Accent:** Butter Yellow (#FFD97D) - Highlights and gamification.
    *   **Neutros:** Bone White (#FAFAFA) for backgrounds, Charcoal (#2D3436) for text.
*   **Typography:** 
    *   **Titles:** Poppins or Quicksand (Rounded, friendly).
    *   **Body:** Inter or DM Sans (Clear, modern).
*   **Design Style:** Minimalist with ample whitespace, soft shadows, and generous border radii (16-24px).

---

## 4. Key Functional Pillars

### A. Dual Marketplace
*   **Official Stores:** Verified badges for professional sellers.
*   **Parent-to-Parent:** Simplified selling for individuals.
*   **Smart Filtering:** Filter by condition (New/Used), age range, price, and brand.

### B. AI-Powered Second-Hand Analysis (Core Innovation)
*   **Guided Photography:** Silhouette-based flow for capturing consistent item views.
*   **Condition Analysis:** Automatic grading (Excellent, Very Good, Good, Used) based on visual data.
*   **Price Suggestion:** Market-data-driven pricing recommendations based on condition.

### C. Smart "Baby Timeline"
*   **Predictive Shopping:** Suggestions based on the baby's age (e.g., 6 months → high chairs; 12 months → walking shoes).
*   **Proactive Notifications:** "Your baby is almost 6 months! Time to look at high chairs?"

### D. Trust & Security (TuKids Guarantee)
*   **Escrow System:** Payments are held until the buyer confirms receipt.
*   **Seller Verification:** ID and selfie verification for increased trust.
*   **Real-Time Tracking:** Live map and step-by-step timeline for order delivery.

---

## 5. Gamification: Seller Levels
*   **Progression:** Bronze → Silver → Gold → Golden Toucan.
*   **Rewards:** Lower transaction fees, featured listing spots, and exclusive badges.

---

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Trust Rating:** Average seller rating and successful escrow completions.
*   **Engagement:** Percentage of users utilizing the Baby Timeline suggestions.
*   **Growth:** Ratio of second-hand vs. new item listings.
